Our Story

A Legacy of Ayurveda Spanning Over 22 Years

The Foundation

Sadguru Kripa Ayurvedic Laboratories has been manufacturing and marketing Ayurvedic oils and wellness products for over 22 years. Built on a foundation of quality, trust, and customer satisfaction, the business has cultivated a loyal customer base and a well-established dealer network across India.

The Evolution

To support future growth and national expansion, the proprietorship was recently reorganised into Sadguru Kripa Ayurvedic Laboratories Pvt. Ltd. — incorporated approximately six months ago. The product portfolio has been strategically rebranded under the Earthleaf identity, creating a modern, scalable, and nationally recognisable brand.

Earthleaf is not a new product line — it is the rebranded evolution of a trusted Ayurvedic business.
